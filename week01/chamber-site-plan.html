Site Name: Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

My purpose with this project is to share information regarding the available business sites surrounding the Idaho Falls area. I want people to know what businesses are available to them and the kind of services they provide. By using this website you should be able to know your options after having recently moved here.

Scenarios

  • Where can I go to open a new checking account?
  • Where do I go if I need to contact Idaho Falls Parks and Recreation?
  • What are my options for entertainment?

Typography

Heading Font: Garamond

This font looks very professional and clean. It would draw me to reading the said content under the heading.

Paragraph Font: Helvetica

Helvetica is just a clean easy reading font. Im using it for my paragraphs so that they can look brighter and nicer. Also Helvetica just sounds cool.

Normal paragraph example

A Chamber of Commerce serves as a valuable resource for businesses in a community, providing networking opportunities, advocacy, and support to foster economic growth and development. Through their programs and initiatives, Chambers of Commerce help businesses connect, collaborate, and navigate the local business landscape, ultimately contributing to the prosperity of the region.

Colored paragraph example

A chamber of commerce can provide businesses with valuable networking opportunities, facilitating connections that can lead to partnerships, collaborations, and increased visibility within the local community. Additionally, chambers of commerce often offer resources and support for businesses, including advocacy, business development programs, and access to educational resources that can contribute to their growth and success.
