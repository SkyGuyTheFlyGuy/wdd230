Population Info

Idaho Falls is a city located in Bonneville County Idaho.

Idaho Falls has a 2023 population of 69,450.

It is also the county seat of Bonneville County.

Idaho Falls is currently growing at a rate of 1.89% annually and its population has increased by 5.78% since the most recent census, which recorded a population of 65,657 in 2020.
